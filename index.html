How to Get Started

6 Easy Steps to Meet Pam

1

Visit Pam Plus

You're already here! When you click "Launch Pam" you'll be asked for your API key. Follow the steps below to get one first.

Link to share: debkirkai.github.io/pam-plus/

2

Create a Free Anthropic Account

Go to console.anthropic.com — Click Sign Up, enter your email, create a password, and click Create Account. It's completely free to sign up.

3

Buy Credits — Start with Just $5

Once logged in, click Billing → Buy Credits → Start with $5. That gives you roughly 150–250 conversations with Pam to start.

Tip: Turn on Auto-Reload so your credits never run out mid-conversation.

4

Get Your API Key

In the console, click API Keys → Create Key → Name it "Pam Plus" → Click Create. Then copy the key immediately — it only shows once!

Keep your key private. It's like a password. Never share it publicly.

5

Paste Your Key Into Pam Plus

Come back here, click Launch Pam, and paste your API key into the box that appears. Click Save. Your key stays in your browser only — it is never shared with anyone.

6

Start Talking to Pam!

Type a message or tap the microphone to speak to Pam. She listens, remembers your whole conversation, and responds like a warm, thoughtful friend.

Bookmark the app page so you go straight to Pam next time — no setup needed!
